Why share your car?

Earn

You offer your car for rental/Hire to your neighbor and EARN handsomely. After getting set up on Ride, most car owners earn enough to cover the costs of owning their cars, including loan monthly payments,fuel,and maintenance.

Freedom

We understand that you need to enjoy the convenience of owning a car, Thus you retain the car and only rent it out when not using it.

Peace of mind

We only allow well screened renters on our platform. We assure you we have done due diligence.Before you approve a rental request, you have a view of the profile of the renter to get comfort.We have your back incase of any dispute.
